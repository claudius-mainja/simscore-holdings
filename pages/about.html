Our Story

Simscore Cleaning Services, a division of Simscore Investments (Pvt) Ltd, was incorporated on 30 December 2004 with the vision of creating a dust-free, safe, and healthy environment for workplaces and homes. Our core business is the provision of contract and on-demand cleaning services for commercial, industrial, institutional, retail, and domestic clients. We specialize in:

  • Floor, wall, and window cleaning
  • Toilet and washroom maintenance
  • Carpet and Upholstery Cleaning
  • Office furniture cleaning
  • Grounds maintenance

With a highly skilled team, advanced equipment, and environmentally friendly cleaning solutions, we are committed to providing reliable, efficient, and professional cleaning services.
