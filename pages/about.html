Our Story

Founded in 2021, Simscore Holdings was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a clean, healthy space to live and work. Based in Harare, Zimbabwe, we've grown into a trusted name in professional cleaning.

We understand that cleanliness goes beyond what meets the eye. It's about creating environments where people feel safe, productive, and comfortable. Whether it's your office, home, or community space, we bring dedication and care to every job.

Our team combines modern cleaning methods with eco-friendly products, ensuring results that are both effective and environmentally responsible.

Our Mission

To raise the standard of cleaning services in Zimbabwe by delivering quality, reliable, and sustainable cleaning solutions that enhance productivity and comfort for businesses and households alike.

Our Vision

To become Zimbabwe's most trusted and preferred cleaning partner, known for excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction.
