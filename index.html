Who We Are

At Simscore Holdings, we believe a clean space is more than just tidy — it's a foundation for success, health, and happiness.

Founded in 2021, Simscore Holdings emerged from a simple belief: every person deserves to live and work in an environment that's not just clean, but truly refreshing. We started with a small team and big dreams, right here in Harare. Today, we've grown into one of Zimbabwe's most trusted cleaning partners, serving hundreds of homes and businesses. Our success comes from treating every space as if it were our own, combining modern cleaning techniques with genuine care and attention to detail.

Our team brings energy, attention to detail, and genuine care to every job. We use modern techniques and eco-friendly products because we believe great results shouldn't come at the expense of our planet.
